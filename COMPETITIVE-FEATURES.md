## VeilVault Unique Differentiators

Unlike competitors, VeilVault provides:

1. **Mathematical Proof** - Not just logs, but cryptographic proofs that records weren't tampered with
2. **Customer Self-Verification** - Bank customers can verify their own statements without trusting the bank
3. **Zero-Knowledge Solvency** - Prove financial health without revealing sensitive numbers
4. **Threshold Operations** - Multi-party cryptographic approval for critical actions
5. **Auditor Independence** - External auditors verify proofs without needing raw data access
